Singapore

My stay in Singapore was not a travel visit, but was during the time I resided there! I lived in Singapore with my family throughout my high school. My stay in Singapore was a memorable experience, one where I experienced living in a new city, and found new and lasting friendships. Other than being a great place for residence and schooling, Singapore has a multitude of tourist attractions, many of which I visited during my stay in the city. Some of these include Universal Studios Singapore, Sentosa beaches, Jewel Changi International Airport, East Coast Beach, Changi Beach, and Marina Bay Sands! All of these places were beautiful and enjoyable. Singapore is home to beaches, malls, nature parks, and great food!

Pulau Ubin

Pulau Ubin is an island northeast of mainland Singapore. Cycle trails and footpaths criss-cross the hills, going past granite quarries and traditional villages. This island is situated just off the north-east coast of Singapore and once was a granite mining hub. It today remains as an abandoned granite quarry, rich in forests and grasslands.

Sentosa Island

Sentosa Island is a small manmade island accessible via a small bridge from main Singapore island, and is illed with entertaining things to do. Sentosa Island contains various attractions such as the Universal Studios, Sentosa Beaches, the Adventure Cove waterpark, the Sentosa Golf Club, and much more. On a day trip to Sentosa, one can visit the beach, hop on some amusement park rudes, visit a waterpark, and enjoy local food options!
