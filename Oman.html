Oman

My visit to Oman during the Summer of 2022 was a unique and memorable one. While staying with my parents at Muscat, we decided to tour the famous tourist spots in Oman. Oman is known for its beautiful nature spots and rich culture. We visited spots on the outskirts of Muscat such as Mattrah. We made a trip down to the scenic and picturesque island of Salalah. Our trips around spots in Oman involved a lot of driving through scenic routes, and enjoying the relaxed ennvironment and the escape from the crowd and fast-paced environment of metrapolitan cities.



Places to Visit in Oman

Salalah

Salalah is the capital city of southern Oman's Dhofar province. It's known for its banana plantations, Arabian Sea beaches, green landscape and seasonal waterfalls. Salalah is very cloudy and foggy during the monsoon months starting from July till August, even though it has relatively little rain falls. Salalah is very picturesque and pleasant, especially during the monsoon, when people make trips to Salalah to get away from the hot and humid weather in the cities. There are many waterfalls to visit, such as at Wadi Darbat, Ain Athum, Ain Tubrook, and Ain Khor.

Royal Opera House Muscat

The Royal Opera House Muscat is Oman's premier venue for musical arts and culture. The opera house is located in Shati Al-Qurm district of Muscat on Sultan Qaboos Street. The Royal Opera House reflects unique contemporary Omani architecture, and has a capacity to accommodate maximum of 1,100 people. The opera house complex consists of a concert theatre, auditorium, formal landscaped gardens, cultural market with retail, luxury restaurants and an art centre for musical, theatrical and operatic productions.
